Frequently Asked Questions!
How to order?
Choose and customize a product to your preference. Make a payment for the product, relax and wait till the product is delivered to your home.
How do I upload my photos for printing?
You can choose to use your Instagram, Facebook, or simply upload from your camera/laptop/computer.
Can I save my order and continue later?
Yes you can continue later; but we only save your picture only in six hour, more then six hour you must start from the beginning.
Can I cancel my order?
Once you've paid for your order then unfortunately no. If you haven't then please inform us by phone to 021-2302002 or through email payment@adorama.co.id
Do I need to create an account to order?
No you don't. You can be a guest and order our products.
I am not happy with my order!
We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee on our products, please inform us with your concerns thru phone at 021-2302002 or email us at printshop@adorama.co.id. We'll find a solution for you.
Can I change my address?
Yes, you need to login to your account and edit your address details through there.
Can I print from my friend's Instagram or from a public hashtag?
No currently you can not. Unless you have their login details :)
Can I delete my photos from Instagram after I order them?
Yes you can, but you may not want to do that as we do not store the photos that we print permanently.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept various types of payment, all major Credit card by Midtrans and Bank Transfer. Unfortunately we don’t do cash on delivery nor Paypal.
How can I check my order status?
You can check your order detail by going to www.jne.co.id and inputting your tracking number.
How do I know that the images I upload have sufficient resolution?
On the website you can find reminder “Image too small”. You must change the picture to get good result.
When can I expect to receive my order?
7-10 working days is the general guideline for the production time of our hand crafted products.
My order arrived damaged! What should I do?
We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee on our products, please inform us what has been damaged through phone at 021-2302002 or email us at hello@printshop.co.id. We'll find a solution for you.
