Shipping Information
Welcome to Printshop shipping information page. We will guide you through a series of short explanation (super short, promise) on how Printshop prepares your order.
Receiving your order
At this point, we’ve received your order and will begin processing it soon—yay! However, before we can start the magic works, we need you to fulfill your order payment as soon as possible. Faster payment means faster order processing!
Confirming your order payment
Don’t forget to confirm your order payment after you fulfill it. Once you’ve done, we will prepare your order right away! Simply click here to fill up the payment confirmation form or you can head down to the bottom of the page and click ‘Confirm payment’ under the footer. Fail to confirm your order in between the provided time might lead into an order cancelation.
Preparing your order
Good news! We’re currently preparing your order. The production time for all photo prints, magnets and canvas will take 1-3 days, while photobook will take 7 days. After that, we will package them safely and nicely on our colorful boxes and envelopes, that we developed especially for you—yes you!
Shipping your package
Let's get excited at this point—it’s the shipment time! To ensure the safety of your package, we partner up with JNE in delivering it. As the one of the largest and most reliable shipping partner, they ensure your package arriving safely in your home in no time. Wait for a little happy knock on your door, okay?
